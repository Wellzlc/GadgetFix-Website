About Gadget Fix LLC

Dallas-Fort Worth's trusted mobile device repair specialists with over 5 years of experience, certified technicians, and a commitment to quality service.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Gadget Fix LLC began with a simple mission: provide fast, reliable, and affordable mobile device repair services to the Dallas-Fort Worth community. What started as a small repair shop has grown into the area's most trusted electronics repair service.

Our founder, Mike Johnson, started repairing devices as a teenager and turned his passion into a profession. With over a decade of experience in electronics repair and a commitment to staying current with the latest technology, Mike built Gadget Fix LLC on the principles of quality workmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Why Choose Gadget Fix LLC?

  • Experience: Over 5 years serving the DFW community with 5,000+ successful repairs
  • Certified Technicians: Our team holds industry certifications and receives ongoing training
  • Quality Parts: We use only OEM and premium aftermarket parts with warranty coverage
  • Fast Service: Most repairs completed same-day with transparent pricing
  • Local Business: Proudly serving our Dallas-Fort Worth neighbors
