Frequently Asked Questions

How long do repairs typically take?

Most common repairs (screen replacement, battery replacement) are completed within 1-2 hours. Complex repairs may take 24-48 hours. We'll provide a time estimate with your quote.

Do you offer warranties on repairs?

Yes! All repairs come with our comprehensive 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If you experience any issues, we'll fix it free of charge.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay).

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended but not required. Walk-ins are welcome, though you may experience longer wait times during busy periods.
